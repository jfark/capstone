What is the Barnes Witz?

A space for you! That is, if you're a teen.

The issue that I am addressing and attempting to solve in my Barnes Witz project is the question of how a museum can reach a next generation of screen-oriented supporters and patrons. This effort is to ensure that The Museum as an institution continues to be seen as relevant and worthwhile, even while competing with the museum of the Internet.
